Our Story ❤️

How We Met

I still remember that morning like it was yesterday. It was during our school's morning sports session. I used to come every day just to play badminton, and that day was your very first time on the court. We were standing on opposite sides, playing against each other, completely unaware that this small moment would one day become the beginning of something so beautiful. Looking back now, I'm really thankful that I decided to come and play badminton that day. ❤️

Our First Conversation

I still remember our very first conversation. It was the beginning of 11th grade. We were already in the same class, but you didn't realize it yet. On the badminton court, you smiled and asked me, "What's your name? Which class are you in?" I laughed and replied, "I'm in your class... try to remember." The moment you realized it, you started laughing, and honestly, I had no idea that such a small conversation would become one of my favourite memories forever. ❤️

Today

Today, we've come so far from those simple school mornings. From strangers... to classmates... to best friends... and finally, to the most beautiful chapter of my life. We've shared smiles, laughter, memories, and even the difficult days. Every single moment has brought us closer. No matter what the future holds, there's one thing I'll always be sure of... If I had the chance to choose again, I'd still choose you. Every single time. Thank you for being the best part of my life. I love you, today, tomorrow, and always. ❤️
